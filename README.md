📱 Sankalpa – Smart Healthcare Assistance App (Android)

Sankalpa is a smart healthcare assistance mobile application designed to simplify daily health management for individuals and families.
The app provides a centralized platform to manage family health profiles, medication reminders, healthcare documents, and access to essential government healthcare information.

Built with a focus on usability, accessibility, and real-life healthcare needs, Sankalpa is especially suitable for elderly users, families, and individuals managing long-term medical care.

🚀 Key Features
👨‍👩‍👧 Family Profile Management

Create and manage multiple family health profiles

Switch active members to personalize the app experience

⏰ Medication Reminder System

Add and manage medication schedules

Track dosage and timing to avoid missed medicines

📷 Camera-Based Face Capture

Integrated camera functionality for face capture

Enables identification and future personalization of profiles

📄 Health Document Management

Securely store and access important medical documents

Keeps prescriptions and reports organized in one place

🏥 Government Healthcare Schemes Access

Quick access to information about healthcare schemes

Includes schemes such as Ayushman Bharat

🚨 Scalable Emergency-Ready Architecture

Designed to easily integrate SOS, location tracking, and emergency services in future versions

🎨 Clean & Intuitive UI/UX

Minimalistic and user-friendly design

Optimized for all age groups, including elderly users
